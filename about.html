Hello! My name is Tyler Bowman and I am currently a student at Nashville Software School. I am in Cohort 22. Before I came to NSS I was a high School U.S. History teacher. I went to Trevecca Nazarene University where I earned a bachelors degree in Music Education.

I gained a love of all things technological from an early age. I am a computer nerd and love video games, especially when they invole puzzles and problem solving.

As a kid I always loved building things. Every birthday or Christmas all I wanted were Legos. I would sit for hours building anything and everything. This love of building has evolved as I have gotten older. Needless to say, if you need a piece of IKEA furniture built, I'm your guy!

I believe that a career as a software developer merges my love of technology, problem solving, and building things. I want to become a software developer because I want to build new and innovative programs to help people and make their lives better.
